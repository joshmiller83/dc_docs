Multiple Instances per Payment Method

In Drupal Commerce, a little known feature is the ability to control payment methods using rules. Rules allows us to play with conditions on line items and/or order totals. For example, you can enable a certain payment system that is designed to handle small orders using a percentage-based fee (PayPal Micro-payments) and enable a different payment system that is designed to handle large orders that is a flat fee.

Another possibility would be to put a condition on a certain payment system based on your customer's location or access rights.
